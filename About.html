I_Med_Et

I_Med_ET stands for “Self-made medicines in Ethiopia” however it sounds like a homophonic phrase similar to “I made it”. It is a website having contents for naturally prepared medicines and herbal remedies to cure a disease employing certain spices, herbs, vegetables, and seeds with minimal or no process. Since there are many indigenous plants used for traditional treatments in Ethiopia, I would like to create this website for sharing tons of excellent content on alternative health & treatments, herbal medicines, alternative medicines with their preparations for a specific disease. This website also supports the Amharic language to overcome the language barrier and address native Amharic speakers.
